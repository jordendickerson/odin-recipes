Chick Fil A Sauce

Description

With just 4 standard household ingredients, you can enjoy Chick-fil-A's® famous dipping sauce--even on Sunday! [1]

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon barbecue sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Mix mayonnaise, yellow mustard, barbecue sauce, and honey together in a small bowl.
